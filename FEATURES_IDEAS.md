# Feature Ideas for Hospital Management System

Ideas you can add to make the system more complete and creative.

---

## Already implemented
- **Homepage** – Landing after login with quick access to Dashboard, Settings, and all modules
- **Settings** – Profile view and change password
- **Role-based UI** – Different home/dashboard for staff vs patient
- **Auto-create Patient** – When a user signs up as Patient, a Patient record is created so they appear in the list

---

## Authentication & security
- **Forgot password** – Reset via email link or OTP
- **Email verification** – Verify email after signup
- **Two-factor authentication (2FA)** – Optional for admin/doctor
- **Session timeout** – Auto logout after inactivity
- **Audit log** – Log who did what (login, create/update patient, etc.)

---

## Notifications & communication
- **In-app notifications** – “New appointment”, “Record added”, etc.
- **Announcements** – Hospital-wide or role-based notices on Homepage
- **Email/SMS reminders** – Appointment reminders to patients
- **Doctor–patient messaging** – Simple secure messaging

---

## Reports & analytics
- **Charts on Dashboard** – Appointments per day, patients per department, etc.
- **Export** – Patients, appointments, or records to CSV/PDF
- **Revenue report** – By department or doctor (if consultation fee is tracked)
- **Occupancy / utilization** – Beds, rooms, or appointment slots

---

## Clinical & operations
- **Billing / invoices** – Generate bills for consultations or procedures
- **Lab results** – Link lab tests to patients and medical records
- **Pharmacy / inventory** – Medicine stock and prescriptions
- **Bed / ward management** – Assign patients to beds, ward occupancy
- **Blood bank** – Donors, inventory, requests
- **Ambulance / emergency** – Log and track emergency requests

---

## Patient experience
- **Book appointment** – Patient can request an appointment (staff/doctor approves)
- **Download medical summary** – PDF of records for the patient
- **Feedback / ratings** – After appointment or discharge
- **Health tips / articles** – Simple CMS on patient portal

---

## Staff & admin
- **Profile edit** – Let user update name (email change with verification)
- **Shift / roster** – Doctor and nurse shifts
- **Leave management** – Apply and approve leave
- **Dashboard widgets** – Customize what appears on Dashboard

---

## UX & design
- **Dark mode** – Theme toggle in Settings
- **Multi-language** – i18n for patient-facing text
- **Pagination** – For long lists (patients, appointments, records)
- **Advanced search** – Filters (date range, department, status) and saved filters

Pick any of these and implement step by step; Homepage + Settings give a good base to extend from.
